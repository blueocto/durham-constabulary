  • 24 July 2018 - Off Road bikes Pelton Fell
    PELTON FELL:  PCSO Walters and residents of Pelton Fell and Waldridge have formed the "Friends of Congburn Woods" group to tackle off road bikes and misuse of the woods. We have a group that can be found on Facebook where updates are posted.
     
    The  aim of this group it to tackle the many issues that have surrounded the woods over the years, this includes off road bikes, fly tipping and general litter problems. This group will be assisting the park rangers in carrying out various tasks to ensure the woods are protected and accessible to all.
    Many community groups and representatives are involved in this group. If you would like to get involved please email PCSO Walters below:
    PCSO 6939 Simon Walters
    Simon.walters@durham.pnn.police.uk
