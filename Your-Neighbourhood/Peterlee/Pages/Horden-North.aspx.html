Active Priorities

  • 30 July 2019 - Empty properties and Anti-Social Behaviour
    Our current PACT priorities are youths causing a nuisance. We are also working with various partners to tackle untidy yards and flytipping in the area.
    We are also aware that off-road vehicles are an issue and we are working with partners to deal with this issue.
    Proctive patrols will be carried out in the area to prevent and deter anti-social behaviour.
    PACT meeting is 8th August at 2pm. This is being held at Horden Hub House on Seventh Street, Horden.
     

This is what we have done

  • 31 August 2018 - Empty properties and Anti-Social Behaviour
    We are working closeley with Durham County Council and other agencies regarding problems in the numbered streets.  Visible patrols daily throughout the area.

These are the policing priorities that local people have agreed with your policing team. You can have some influence on future policing priorities by attending your next PACT meeting.
